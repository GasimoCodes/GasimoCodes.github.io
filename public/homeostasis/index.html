You're humanity's only boots on the ground.

A frozen wasteland stretches endlessly before you. Sent to a remote Antarctic region, you're tasked with a critical mission: assess its habitability for future colonization.

Manage resources, document key sites, and survive long enough to finish the job and return home.

Outfitted with a mechanical arm and vital instrumentation, it's your most valuable tool in the snow.

Balance fuel, heat, and power to keep both you and your vehicle operational.
