Roxxy & Ewe

FULL DECK!

“Oh no, its these two”

Join Roxxy & Ewe on their whimsical quest to journey through the world. One is in for the thrill, the other strives to spread kindness. Although they may not agree on everything, one thing they share:

Both are utterly underqualified for adventuring.

But together, they just may overcome even the biggest challenges.

The City with no Cards

Our unconventional adventurers find themselves stuck in a vibrant, idyllic kingdom where card games are banned. As Roxxy's beloved deck is confiscated, it's clear that the kingdom's gravest threat isn't from some mythical beasts or draconic foes, but from the sheer boredom its residents are now forced to endure.

Can our duo sway the royal family to lift the ban and retrieve Roxxy's cards back? Can they do that while keeping a moral high ground? And what about the dragon?

Switcheroo!

Some problems require wits, some means and some both. Toggle seamlessly between Roxxy and Ewe to navigate through the oddest situation, craziest dialogue or the trickiest puzzle.
